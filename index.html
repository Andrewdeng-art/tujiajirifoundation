The Unemployment Challenge in Kenya

Unemployment in Kenya continues to affect millions, especially the youth and marginalized groups. The lack of job opportunities, limited access to career guidance, and inadequate vocational training have left many struggling to find sustainable income sources.

Our mission is to bridge this gap by connecting individuals to employment services, job training programs, entrepreneurship opportunities, and support networks. We aim to empower the unemployed by providing information, resources, and a platform for growth.

Our Solutions

We offer practical tools and partnerships to help individuals access employment opportunities and improve their skills.

Skills Training

We provide free and subsidized vocational and digital training programs tailored to the job market.

Job Matching

Our platform connects job seekers with employers, internships, and volunteering opportunities.

Career Guidance

We offer career counseling and CV writing support to help individuals present themselves professionally.

Entrepreneur Support

For those who want to start their own businesses, we provide mentorship and access to startup resources.

Unlocking Potential. Restoring Hope.

Together we help unemployed individuals rebuild their lives with dignity and purpose.
